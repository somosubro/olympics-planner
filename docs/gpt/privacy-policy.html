Privacy Policy — Olympics Schedule Planner (Custom GPT)

Last updated: April 6, 2026

This Custom GPT helps you explore Olympic session schedules and plans. It uses Actions to call an HTTPS API (for example on Google Cloud Run) to retrieve sessions, validate plans, and rank options.

What is sent to the API

When you use the GPT, the assistant may send requests to that API. Those requests can include:

  • Filters derived from your questions (e.g. sports, days, dates).
  • Structured data such as preferences (sports, days, rules) and plans (session IDs), as required for validation and ranking.

This GPT is not intended to collect passwords, payment card numbers, or government ID numbers.

Who processes data

OpenAI’s privacy policy applies to ChatGPT and Custom GPTs. Your API / cloud provider processes HTTP traffic to your backend under their terms and your account.
